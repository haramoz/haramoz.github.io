Dr. Sattlers Bar

It is a microservice based Full-stack solution for a fictional medieval bar, name Dr. Sattlers Bar. The technology stack is Java, Springboot, React, Docker, Kubernetes, Kafka, Html5, CSS3, Junit. It is a tribute to the dr. sattlers bar, that I used to manage for 2 years during my glorious student days.
